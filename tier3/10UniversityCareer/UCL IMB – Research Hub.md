## IB Entry Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **IB Diploma** | 38 points overall |
| **HL Requirements** | 18 points across 3 HL subjects (no score below 5) |
| **Maths AA HL** | 6+ required |
| **English B HL** | 7 required |
| **Science or Social Science HL** | Preferred |

**Note**: Math HL 5 meets the minimum but is borderline. Personal statement quality, tech-business experience, and overall IB points are the critical compensating factors.

## Application Strategy

### Personal Statement

Start drafting now — the earlier the better. Focus on demonstrable tech-business interest: specific projects, tools, reading, experiences.

**Key angles**:
- Developer background: IB Gazette CMS, iOS apps, AI projects
- Business awareness: Business SL, entrepreneurial mindset
- Information systems crossover: AI tools, workflow automation, data
- International profile: multilingual (Greek/English), Athens-based, global outlook

### Timeline

- Submit early in the UCAS cycle (October/November if possible)
- UCL IMB is oversubscribed — early submission is an advantage
- Equal consideration deadline is January, but earlier helps
- Target: Application submitted 1–7 October 2026

### Reference Points

- Omilia internship (tech/business crossover)
- MIT FutureBuilders bootcamp
- IB Gazette + CGSMUN
- AI hackathon award
- Portfolio / GitHub linked in PS

## Bocconi BEMACS Alternative

As EU prestige alternative to UCL IMB:

- **Degree**: BSc Economics, Management and Computer Science (BEMACS)
- **Location**: Bocconi University, Milan, Italy
- **Duration**: 3 years
- **Admission**: BOT score (55%) + Years 10–11 pre-IB GPA (45%)
- **Target BOT**: 35+/50 (40+ = strong for BEMACS)
- **Fees**: ~€14,300/year
- **Round 1 Deadline**: September

**Strategy**: Run in parallel with UK applications — do not wait for UCL outcome.
