# Exam Strategy – All Subjects – Meta

Meta-strategies for IB exams across all subjects.

## General Principles

### Time Management

- Calculate: (total marks × 1.5) = minutes available
- Don't spend too long on any single question
- Attempt everything – partial credit exists

### Command Terms

| Term | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| Define | Give meaning |
| Explain | Show why/how |
| Describe | Give details |
| Analyze | Break down components |
| Evaluate | Judge with criteria |
| Discuss | Present multiple views |

## Study Timeline

1. **T-6 months**: Review all content, make summary notes
2. **T-3 months**: Practice past papers
3. **T-1 month**: Focus on weak areas, timed practice
4. **T-1 week**: Light review, rest, avoid burnout

## Exam Day

- Read questions carefully
- Answer what's asked, not what you prepared
- Manage anxiety – breathing exercises
