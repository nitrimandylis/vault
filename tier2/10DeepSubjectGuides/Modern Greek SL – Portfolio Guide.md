## Assessment Components

### Individual Oral
- Commentary on one literary + one non-literary text
- Global issue linking both texts
- 15 min presentation + questions

### Paper 1 — Guided Analysis
- Unseen text analysis
- Guided questions

### Paper 2 — Comparative Essay
- Essay comparing two works from portfolio
