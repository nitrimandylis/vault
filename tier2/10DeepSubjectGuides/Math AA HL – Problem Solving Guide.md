# Math AA HL – Problem Solving Guide

Problem-solving strategies for Mathematics AA HL.

## Topic Breakdown

### Algebra

- Binomial theorem, sequences, series
- Complex numbers
- Proof by induction

### Functions

- Domain, range, inverse functions
- Transformations
- Rational functions

### Trigonometry

- Unit circle, identities
- Solving trig equations
- 3D trigonometry

### Calculus

- Differentiation (product, quotient, chain rule)
- Integration (substitution, parts)
- Kinematics, differential equations

### Statistics & Probability

- Probability distributions
- Hypothesis testing
- Correlation, regression

## Problem-Solving Strategies

1. Identify knowns and unknowns
2. Choose appropriate approach
3. Show all working clearly
4. Check answers against original problem
