# English B HL – Reading & Writing Playbook

Strategies for English B HL.

## Paper 1: Writing

### Task Types

- Discursive essay
- Argumentative essay
- Article
- Email/Letter

### Structure

1. Introduction – context + thesis
2. Body paragraphs – arguments with evidence
3. Conclusion – synthesis, no new points

### Tips

- Use varied vocabulary (not just impressive words, but precise ones)
- Cohesion devices: furthermore, however, consequently
- Stay within word count

## Paper 2: Reading

### Skills Tested

- Comprehension
- Analysis of authorial choices
- Synthesis between texts

### Approach

1. Read questions first
2. Skim for relevant sections
3. Quote to support answers
4. Time management: 1.5 min per mark

## Oral Assessment

- Prepare for known questions
- Speak for required duration
- Show cultural understanding
