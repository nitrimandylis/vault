# EE – Project Hub

Extended Essay — Business Management.

## Research Question

**To what extent has NVIDIA's strategic shift from gaming-oriented hardware to AI-focused solutions impacted its market dominance, financial success, profitability and reputation?**

## Framework Selection

- SWOT Analysis + Competitor Analysis (AMD, Intel, Qualcomm)
- Product Life Cycle: gaming GPU curve vs. AI accelerator curve
- Ansoff Matrix: Product Development and Diversification quadrants
- Porter's Five Forces: competitive dynamics in AI hardware
- Financial Analysis: 5-year ratios + benchmarking vs. AMD and Intel

## Essay Structure

1. Introduction
2. Methodology
3. Situational Analysis: SWOT, Competitor Analysis, Product Life Cycle
4. Strategic Analysis: Ansoff Matrix, Porter's Five Forces
5. Financial Analysis 2019–2024: Ratio Table, Revenue Segment Breakdown, Benchmarking
6. Conclusion
7. Bibliography
