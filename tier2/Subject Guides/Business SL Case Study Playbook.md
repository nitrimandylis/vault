# Business SL – Case Study Playbook

Approach for Business Management case studies.

## Key Frameworks

### SWOT Analysis

- Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats

### Porter's Five Forces

- Barriers to entry, supplier power, buyer power, substitutes, rivalry

### Financial Ratios

- Profitability: Gross margin, net margin, ROCE
- Liquidity: Current ratio, quick ratio
- Efficiency: Inventory turnover, debtor days

## Command Terms

- **Define** – Give meaning
- **Explain** – Show why/how
- **Analyze** – Break down, discuss implications
- **Evaluate** – Judge with criteria
- **Recommend** – Suggest with justification

## Case Study Approach

1. Read quickly for overview
2. Identify key issues
3. Apply relevant frameworks
4. Make balanced recommendation

## Internal Assessment

- Research a real business
- Apply course concepts
- Make justified recommendations
