# TOK – Essay & Exhibition Toolkit

Resources for TOK assessment.

## Ways of Knowing (WOKs)

- Sense perception
- Reason
- Emotion
- Memory
- Imagination
- Intuition
- Faith
- Language

## Areas of Knowledge (AOKs)

- Mathematics
- Natural sciences
- Human sciences
- History
- The arts
- Ethics
- Religious knowledge systems
- Indigenous knowledge systems

## Essay Guidance

### Choosing a Title

- Pick something you can connect to 2+ AOKs
- Ensure you can provide real examples

### Structure

1. Introduction – Present the claim
2. Body – 3-4 paragraphs with counterclaims
3. Conclusion – Synthesize perspectives

### Key Tips

- Use real examples (not hypotheticals)
- Discuss limitations of knowledge
- Show personal engagement

## Exhibition

- Object + commentary (950 words max)
- Link to one WOK and one AOK
- Show real-world application
