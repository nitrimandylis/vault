# CAS – Core Activities Hub

## Overview

150+ hours total (approximately 50 hours each):

## Requirements

- At least one activity from each category
- Must show learning outcomes
- Reflections required throughout

5. Demonstrate the skills and recognize the benefits of working collaboratively
6. Recognize and consider the ethics of choices and actions

## Activity Reflections

### Kizuna Initiative
Co-founded student committee with 2 classmates. Three pillars: CAS coordination platform, subject clubs during study periods, student council event collaboration. Built website + pitch deck, presented to school leadership. Still in progress. *Key insight: gap between identifying a problem and being trusted to solve it. Service need makes creativity viable.*

### ACSTAC AI Hackathon (ClearFeed)
Developed ClearFeed — AI-powered fact-checker browser extension for teen social media users. Finalist at ACSTAC Thessaloniki. *Key insight: constraints force decisions; communication work makes technical ideas land with general audiences.*

### ACSTAC Exhibition (ASKUS)
Presented ASKUS drone delivery at Thessaloniki. Left without award — aerospace/drone category had no sector-specific prize. *Key insight: effort and outcome not proportional. ASKUS still earned AI Hackathon finalist + IST top 50. One data point, not a verdict.*

### IST Crazy Business Ideas (ASKUS)
ASKUS: hybrid VTOL drone delivery targeting elderly/rural communities in Greece. Top 50 from 130 teams. Developed full business plan, ran primary research, iterated multiple drafts. *Key insight: stress-test assumptions before committing — exposed gaps in logistics costs + regulatory barriers.*
