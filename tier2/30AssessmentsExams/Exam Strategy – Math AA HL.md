# Exam Strategy – Math AA HL

Exam preparation for Math AA HL.

## Papers Overview

### Paper 1 (2 hours)

- No calculator
- Short and long questions
- Pure math topics

### Paper 2 (2 hours)

- GDC (calculator) required
- All topics including stats/probability

### Paper 3 (1 hour) – HL Only

- Short questions
- Complex numbers, calculus

## Study Approach

1. **Review problem solving guide**: [[Math AA HL – Problem Solving Guide]]
2. **Formula sheet** – Know what's on it, what isn't
3. **Practice past papers** – Time yourself

## Key Topics

- Differentiation (product, quotient, chain rule)
- Integration (substitution, by parts)
- Complex numbers
- Proof by induction
- Binomial theorem

## Tips

- Show all working for partial credit
- Know GDC shortcuts
- Don't skip marks – attempt everything
- Unit checks save marks
