# IB Mindset & Rules

My guiding principles for surviving and thriving through the IB Diploma.

## Mindset

- **Finish what matters most first** – Prioritize HLs and IA/EE
- **Sustainability over intensity** – 2-year marathon, not a sprint
- **Balance CAS + academics** – Both are required, both matter
- **Internal assessment is key** – IA grades matter more than you think

## Non-Negotiables

- Sleep 7+ hours
- Exercise 3x per week minimum
- Daily breaks from screen work
- Weekly social connection

## Academic Rules

- Start IA/EE at least 6 months before deadline
- Revise with past papers, not just re-reading notes
- Teach concepts to verify understanding
- Use spaced repetition for memorization
